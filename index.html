Scheda Tecnica

How to Use Glue + Hardener

Always mix both components fully before use. Follow the ratio on your product label for the variant and pack size selected.

⚗ System Two-part — Glue + Hardener (not single-component)
◐ Solid Variants White · Beige · Transparent — paste consistency
💧 Liquid Variant Transparent — flowing formula for impregnation & coating
📦 Solid Pack Sizes White & Beige: 1 kg · 5 kg · 23 kg  |  Transparent: 1 kg · 5 kg · 18 kg
📦 Liquid Pack Sizes Transparent: 1 L · 5 L · 18 L
🔗 Also Available Joint Adhesive 100 ml · AB Epoxy 1 kg

Application Process

01

Prepare

Surfaces must be clean, dry, and free of dust, oil, and loose material. Sand smooth for the best bond.

02

Mix

Combine glue and hardener in the correct ratio. Mix thoroughly with a paddle until fully homogeneous — incomplete mixing weakens the bond.

03

Apply

Spread solid formulas with a trowel or spatula. Pour or brush liquid transparent where penetration or coating is needed.

04

Cure

Allow full cure before polishing, loading, or exposing to traffic. Do not mix more than you can apply within the working time.
